# Product Marketing Context: 3PPS.info

## What We Do

3PPS.info is a free educational resource about USPTO Third-Party Preissuance Submissions — the statutory mechanism under 35 U.S.C. § 122(e) that lets any member of the public place prior art directly in front of a patent examiner before a patent is granted.

The site demystifies an obscure but powerful patent system tool using plain English, practical tools, and honest strategic analysis.

## Core Value Proposition

Most people who need to challenge a pending patent don't know this tool exists, and lawyers rarely explain it for free. 3PPS.info is the plain-English guide that:
- Explains what 3PPS is, how it works, and what it costs (often $0)
- Provides a deadline calculator (the window can close without warning)
- Walks through the drafting rules (no legal arguments — common rejection reason)
- Gives honest strategic analysis (filing can backfire by strengthening the patent)
- Compares US 3PPS to EPO and PCT equivalents

## Target Audience (ICP)

**Primary:** Tech companies and startups who see a competitor's pending patent application and want to submit prior art before it issues. Often engineers or in-house counsel doing initial research.

**Secondary:** Individual inventors, patent professionals (attorneys/agents), academics, and policy-focused researchers who want to understand the mechanism.

**What triggers them:** Spotting a published patent application that could threaten their product, market, or freedom to operate.

**What they need:** Quick clarity on eligibility, deadline urgency, what they can and cannot submit, and whether it's worth doing vs. saving the art for an IPR.

## Positioning

- **Not a legal service** — educational only, explicitly recommends consulting a practitioner
- **Honest about tradeoffs** — the strategy page says "just because you can doesn't mean you should"
- **Practical over theoretical** — tools (calculator, checklist) over dense legal text
- **Anonymous-friendly** — highlights that 3PPS allows anonymous filing

## Key Messages

1. The filing window is "brittle" — it can close with no warning when a Notice of Allowance issues
2. First-time filings with ≤3 documents are free ($0)
3. You cannot argue with the Examiner — factual descriptions only
4. Filing can backfire if the applicant narrows around your prior art
5. 3PPS is not a pre-grant opposition — your role ends after filing

## Tone & Voice

Plain English, precise, non-alarmist. Explains complexity without dumbing it down. Neutral and educational — not advocacy. Includes appropriate disclaimers (not legal advice) without being preachy about it.

## Lead Generation

Primary lead magnet: "Free 3PPS Compliance Checklist" PDF (one-page, covers required elements, rejection reasons, fee exemption rules). Captured via email form on homepage.

## Competitive Landscape

- USPTO's own documentation (dense, hard to parse)
- Law firm blog posts (often incomplete or behind a contact form)
- No direct competitor focused purely on 3PPS education with free tools

## Technology

Static site built with Eleventy (11ty), deployed via Netlify. Lightweight, no CMS.
